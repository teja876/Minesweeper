Minesweeper

Instructions

  • Game contains hidden mines in random cells.
  • Game contains 9 * 9 dimensions and 10 mines
  • You are provided with only 10 flags to mark any cell as bomb
  • left click on any cell to reveal the no of mines on its neibours. right click on any cell to mark it as Flag
  • If you click on any cell which contains mines they you lose
  • Reveal all the cell without clicking any mines.
  • To make the game easy for you we reveal all the cells which does not contain any neighbour mines if you click on such cells.
